In most cases, Toyota recommends that Toyota owners schedule a service appointment every six months or 5000 miles, whichever comes first. The maintenance performed during each service appointment varies by vehicle model and year, driving conditions and other factors that our trained technicians evaluate. However, recommended maintenance often includes services such as tire rotation,oil change and inspection of wiper blades, brake pads and fluid levels.
